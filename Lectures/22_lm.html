The Problem of Multiple Comparisons

Solutions to Multiple Comparisons?

  1. Ignore it - a test is a test Least Squares Difference test
  1. Lower your \(\alpha\) given m = # of comparisons
    • Bonferroni \(\alpha/m\)
    • False Discovery Rate \(k\alpha/m\) where k is rank of test
  1. Other multiple comparinson correction
    • Tukey’s Honestly Significant Difference
